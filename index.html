AmI (Ambient Intelligence) Lab

AmI Lab is in CSE at the University of Michigan led by Prof. Ke Sun. We build AI-native computational sensing systems for mobile, wearable, IoT, and robotic platforms. Our research centers on three questions:

  • How can off-the-shelf sensors be repurposed to achieve extended sensing capabilities?
  • How can we address the systems challenges of emerging AI-native sensing systems?
  • How can sensing systems be designed to better support everyday life?

Recent Research Highlight

  • RFCanvas: Modeling RF Channel by Fusing Visual Priors and Few-shot RF Measurements (SenSys'24)
  • UltraPoser: IMU-based Full-Body Pose Estimation with Ultrasound Sensing on Consumer Wearables (UIST'25)
  • EveGuard: Defeating Vibration-based Side-Channel Eavesdropping with Audio Adversarial Perturbations (S&P'25)
